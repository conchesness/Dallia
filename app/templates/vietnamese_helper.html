How to address people in Vietnamese

# Vietnamese Word Audio Meaning
1 Bac Pronounced "Back" Gender neutral term used for the elderly
2 Anh Say the word "and" but without the d Used for males that are old enough to be your sibilings
3 Ba What a lamb would say What you would call your grandma
4 Ong What you would call your grandpa
5 Em What you would call yourself to someone who is your age
